Extracts from School Log (From 1971….)

1971

May 14th

Infant classes occupied rooms at new school
   

2 Infant classes to remain at original school

1972

Sept.

Final infant classes move to new site

1973

Sept.

Number of children on roll: 301
 

Nov. 13th

School closed for Princess Anne’s Wedding to Capt Mark Phillips

1974

Jan. 9th

Gales caused damage to roof of school building
 

Aug. 31st

Mr J Peyman retires
 

Sept. 1st

Ivor Arthur Simmons appointed head teacher
   

Number of children on roll: 311

 

Oct. 11th

Party of Class 3 children visit Fairford to see Concorde

1975

Jan.

Number of children on roll: 323

1976

Jan.

Storm damage of terrapin classrooms
 

Aug. 31st

A G Chambers (deputy head) terminates appointment
 

Sept. 1st

R Wensley appointed new deputy head teacher
   

Number of children on roll: 280
   

Kingshill School opens
 

Dec. 31st

Ivor Simmons terminates appointment

1977

Jan. 1st

Margaret P Bennett appointed as head teacher
 

June 3rd

Half day holiday for Queen’s Silver Jubilee
 

Aug. 31st

R Wensley terminates appointment
 

Sept. 1st

R L Jefferies appointed new deputy head

1981

Sept.

Shire Hall advise that log books entries are no longer required. However, Miss Bennett continues to log events for future reference

1985

Spring Term

Architects appointed for new school building. Proposal 4 classrooms – 5 requested to replace terrapins which will be demolished

1986

Spring Term

Phase I building work begins. Original building contractors go bankrupt – new contractors have to be found

1987

Jan 15th 16th

School closed due to heavy snow
 

Sept.

New Infant building completed

1988

Sept.

Playgroup move to school site
 

Dec. 31st

Mr Jefferies terminates appointment

1989

Jan. 1st

Mr A Duffy appointed new deputy head
 

SummerTerm

Phase II of building work well under way
 

Dec. 12th

Christmas performances cancelled due to influenza epidemic

1990

Feb. 27th

Mr J Peyman’s funeral
 

Mar. 9th

Miss Ros Jordan (teacher) killed whilst cycling home from school
 

April

Demolition of old school as new building completed
 

Sept.

Infant adventure playground started
   

Number of children on roll: 300
 

Oct. 12th

School officially opened by Pam Ayres

1991

March 19th

Dedication of Miss Jordan’s garden
 

April

Mrs J Arnold appointed as teacher
 

Aug. 31st

Miss Bennett terminates appointment
 

Sept 1st

Mr Lewis appointed as head teacher
