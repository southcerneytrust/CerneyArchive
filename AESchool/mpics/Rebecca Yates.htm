Ann Edwards was a kind and honest lady. Her mother was the sister of John Jones and she was also known as Widow House of Bibury. Ann Edwards ran a dame school teaching reading and writing. Ann Edwards married the Rev. Isaac Edwards. In 1815 something strange happened to Ann and her husband. 11 of their relatives died in one year. Then in 1819 Isaac Edwards died after wearing wet clothes in church and Ann Edwards was left very wealthy.

She spent her money on lots of things. She gave money to have the clock built on the church. She gave £1,000 to the poor in South Cerney. She gave the money to have the National School and the school house built and she gave the rest of her money to help widows and orphans of church families.

Rebecca Yates
