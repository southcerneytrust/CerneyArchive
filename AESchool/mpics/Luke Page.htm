Mrs Ann Edwards lived in Compton Casey, then she went with her mother to live in South Cerney. She married Rev. Isaac Edwards and she was poor but kind, helpful and caring. She helped people and she was nice. Eleven of her relatives died in one year. In

1819 the Rev. Isaac Edwards, her husband, died after he went to hear the bishop speak in Cirencester. He sat in church in wet clothes and he died because he had lung problems.

Luke Page
