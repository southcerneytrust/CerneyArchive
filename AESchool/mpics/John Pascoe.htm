Ann Edwards parents were Thomas Hayward of Compton Casey and Widow House of Bibury. She was very kind to almost everyone. Ann Edwards got married to Reverend Isaac Edwards and they got money from eleven of their relatives who died in 1815.

One day Isaac went to church in Cirencester. It was raining when he got to church and he was so wet he caught a cold and died. Before 1820 Ann Edwards ran a Dame School. After her husband died she gave the money to build a new school, she bought a clock for the church and she paid all her father's debts. She died in 1834 and some of the money she left paid for the building of Edwards College.

John Pascoe
