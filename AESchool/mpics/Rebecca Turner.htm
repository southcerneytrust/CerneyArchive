This is the village Hall. The roof is thatched to stop birds and things like that making nests. A long time ago the Romans went past Upper Mill and along a road where the Village Hall is now.

There is a bench next to the hall in memory of Margaret Franklin .The village hall has no chimney, but it has a cloakroom

and a toilet. You can do activities there. It has 4 windows. The village hall used to be a barn.

Rebecca Turner
