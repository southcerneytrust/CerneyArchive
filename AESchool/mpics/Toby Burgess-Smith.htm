The head and foot are part of an old crucifix that was sealed in the wall of the church because people were taking any pictures or statues of Jesus out of churches. They were found in one of the walls and there are still some marks on the wall where they were found. The pieces in the church are copies of the original pieces which are now in the British Museum because they are so old and so important.
