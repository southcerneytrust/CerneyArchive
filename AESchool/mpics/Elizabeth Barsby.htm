Mrs Ann Edwards ran a Dame School and she taught children to read and write. The school was opened in 1820 and it was a Church of England Foundation School. Later on Mrs Ann Edwards gave £1,000 to build a school house for a school master and a school mistress. In 1868 the west wing was built on the school and it was opened in August 1869. Mrs Ann Edwards died in 1834.

 

Elizabeth Barsby
