The main aim of the South Cerney Millennium CD-ROM project is to provide residents of the village with copies of local photographs and other records that are easy to keep and simple to access.

Our grateful thanks go to the many individuals and organisations who allowed us to share their pictures.

The best way to find material on the CD-ROM is through the Contents view on the left of the screen. Alternatively, try the Search View if you know what you're looking for.

As a taster, browse Local Pictures from a map of South Cerney or try Maps and Aerial Views to see a birds-eye of the village.

Click on Ann Edwards School Work to see what the primary school children have been up to this year.  Look in the Contents view and you will also find Ann Edwards School archive photographs from 1907 to the present day.

Click on Millennium Self-Portrait to see movie clips from Raymond Fenton's and Ann Turner's impressive film about life in the village.
