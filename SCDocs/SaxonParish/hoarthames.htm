Anglo-Saxon 

English
8. Of Nunnera Pol on Haran Stan.

9. Of Haran Stan on thronne Haran Withig.

10. Of Haran Withig innan Temese.

8. From Nun's Stream to the Hoar Stone.

9. From the Hoar Stone to the Hoar Willow.

10. From the Hoar Willow to the Thames.

 

 

 

 

 

The stream follows an artificial path.  Presumably the stone marked the boundary on the stream before the course was altered.

 

It must be near to where the stream meets the Thames c 1/2 mile SSW of Latton.
