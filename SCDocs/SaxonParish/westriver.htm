Parish Boundary Location 12                 

Anglo-Saxon 

English
Andlang Hodes Lace up on thaet Ea Demn* the man haet West ea. Along Hod's Lake up to that river called West River.

 

 

 

 

*If denoting a place the meaning is obscure.  West River is, it seems, a name applied to the stream noted previously, which runs N-S, where it passes just west of the village, ie a local name for a stretch of a longer stream.
