South Cerney Exhibition


 

 

 

From the earliest days of human presence in this area, at least 40,000 BC, the fertile land of the Thames valley, and thus of South Cerney and Cerney Wick, attracted hunters, gatherers, cultivators and eventually settlers.

 

All left behind, within the boundaries of the modern parish, evidence of their life, work and death. Aerial photographs reveal burial barrows, field enclosures, and trackways.
