From the replies in this section ninety people attend a place of worship weekly of which just over half go to the Anglican church of All Hallows, a handful attend the United church and the remainder go to other places of worship. A third of us never attend, whilst the remainder are split between monthly, a few times a year and less than once a year.


Regarding the importance of the churches and chapel the majority felt they were important for baptisms,  weddings and funerals and as an historical building and for congregational or private worship.  Half thought every village should have one.
