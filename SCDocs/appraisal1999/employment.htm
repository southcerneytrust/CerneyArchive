The village news on the employment front is very good, with nearly half of us in full-time employment, approximately 10 % in part time, and a similar number are self-employed. Nearly one fifth of villagers are retired and unemployment level is less than 1.4 %.

  
 

Half of the villagers work in the professional/managerial field, whilst the spread between unskilled, semi-skilled, skilled manual and clerical/supervisory, is even.


The majority travel to work by car, van or motorcycle. Only a tenth use the bus, 170 walk or cycle, 25 go by train and 8 folk use other means!? There are two peaks in the distances travelled, namely 1-5 miles and 11-25 miles, and these probably correspond with the major centres of employment and education at Cirencester and Swindon respectively.
