Rather sadly our mobile public library service is used by few readers just 4% on a regular basis, and a massive 92% never use it. However, encouragingly, our village magazine chiefly, along with the Wilts. and Glos. Standard, are very popular and are read on a regular basis.

The majority view on the village notice board is that the contents are reasonable to good and that reading it once a month is most popular.

The withdrawal of the public telephone kiosk would cause major problems for 3%, but the vast majority would appear to be well equipped in this era of mobile communications.
